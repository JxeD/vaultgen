Write For Us

Are you passionate about cybersecurity, password management, and online safety? VaultGen is looking for talented writers to contribute to our blog. Share your expertise and help our readers stay secure in the digital world.

Topics We Cover

  • Cybersecurity best practices
  • Password management tips

Submission Guidelines

To ensure high-quality content, please follow these guidelines:

  • Articles should be original and not published elsewhere.
  • Word count: 800-1500 words.
  • Include actionable tips and insights.
  • Use proper grammar and formatting.

How to Submit

Email your article or pitch to submissions@vaultgen.com. Our team will review your submission and get back to you within 5-7 business days.
